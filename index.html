Tribute to Tribute

"The Greatest Song in the World"

"Tribute" was written as a paean to the impossibility of reaching musical perfection.

"Tribute" was released on February 11, 2002, in the United States and on June 24, 2002, in Australia.

"Tribute" is a song by American comedy rock duo Tenacious D, and the second single from their self-titled debut album (2001).

The song is a "Tribute" to what Kyle Gass and Jack Black refer to as "The Greatest Song in the World" (often confused as the song's title).

"Tribute" was the first song Black and Gass played live as Tenacious D. The song, like many other songs that were recorded on Tenacious D, was originally performed on their short-lived HBO TV series.
